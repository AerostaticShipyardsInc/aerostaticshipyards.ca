Technology

Our aircraft are built around our Hybrid Thermostatic Dirigible architecture, designated HTD.

HTD is our in-house approach to modern buoyancy management and a streamlined onboard energy arrangement, designed to support stable long-duration operation and practical mission handling.

Technical implementation details and performance specifications are shared in appropriate technical or partnership contexts.

Platforms

Aerostatic Shipyards is developing a family of lighter-than-air aircraft based on the HTD architecture.

HTD-U is an unmanned aircraft under development for persistent sensing, monitoring, and operational support missions.

HTD-1 is a later-stage platform concept focused on passenger flight and extended operational capability.
