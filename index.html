The New Airshipwrights

We are bringing buoyant flight back to the modern world. Aerostatic Shipyards designs sustainable airships and hybrid lift systems that unite proven lighter than air principles with clean fuel innovation. Our focus is quiet operation, low emissions, and endurance suitable for real commercial missions.

HTD 1 is a thermostatic hybrid airship that combines a helium envelope with internal heated air ballonets to control lift without ballast or helium venting. A single propane supply powers propulsion and the heating system. The result is stable engine off hovering, very low noise, and precise altitude control for tourism, observation, and research.
