The New Airshipwrights

We are bringing buoyant flight back to the modern world. Aerostatic Shipyards is contributing to Canada’s entry into the renewed global wave of lighter than air innovation by developing sustainable aircraft that pair proven buoyant flight principles with modern clean-fuel technology. Our work focuses on quiet operation, low emissions, and the endurance required for real commercial missions, from tourism and research to regional access and environmental observation.

HTD-1 is a next-generation lighter than air aircraft that uses a refined lift management approach to adjust buoyancy without routine venting of lifting gas or reliance on heavy ballast. A unified onboard energy system supports both propulsion and controlled buoyancy modulation, enabling stable low-speed flight, low acoustic impact, and precise positioning. The platform is being developed for passenger, observation, and research missions where quiet operation and minimal infrastructure are essential.
